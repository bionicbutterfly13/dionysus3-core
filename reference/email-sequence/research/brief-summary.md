### Solution Mechanism: Predictive Identity Recalibration

Three layers:
- **Layer 1:** Redirect Replay Loop from evidence-gathering into strategic pattern recognition
- **Layer 2:** Integrate Avatar and Self into single presence (eliminate energy drain)
- **Layer 3:** Redesign external ecosystem (calendar, team, protocols) to sustain integration
