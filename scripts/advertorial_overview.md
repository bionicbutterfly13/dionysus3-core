**Purposes of Advertorial:**

> • Do The Legwork Of Selling a Product/Service
>
> • Sell The Click (To a VSL, PDP, Etc)
>
> • Compliance (Interstitial)
>
> • Maintain Congruence Between Ad and Landing Page (PDP, VSL, Etc)

**Common Elements:**

> • Educational (About Unique Mechanism and/or the Product)
>
> • Personal (Emotional)
>
> • Curiosity-Driven

**Common Lengths:**

> • 200-500 Words (Short)
>
> • 500-1,000 Words (Medium)
>
> • 1,000 - 2,000 Words (Long)
