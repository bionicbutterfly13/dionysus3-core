**Step 4: Rewrite the OODA Loop**
Your current [[OODA Loops and Heartbeat Agent]]:
1. **Observe:** Ambiguous social cue
2. **Orient:** *Skipped* (jump straight to Decide)
3. **Decide:** Must fix/flee/fight
4. **Act:** Defensive response

**Replay Loop OODA:**
1. **Observe:** Ambiguous social cue (same)
2. **Orient:** "What basin am I in? Is this threat real or thoughtseed activation?"
3. **Decide:** "Do I act on threat-seed or alternative seed?"
4. **Act:** Chosen response (not reflex)

You insert the ORIENT phase. That's the entire intervention.

**Step 5: Build Alternative Basins**

Replay successful episodes → strengthen alternative thoughtseeds → carve new attractor basin.
